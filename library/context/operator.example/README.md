# Operator context — setup

These are YOUR personal files: positioning, profile, design-language, and voice. On first run, copy this folder to `library/context/operator/` (gitignored — your content stays private) and fill in each one from its `.example`.

| File | What it is | How to fill it |
|---|---|---|
| `positioning.example.md` | Your strategic stance / content pillars | Replace the bracketed prompts. |
| `profile.example.md` | Who you are (operator-self) | Replace the bracketed prompts. |
| `design-language.example.md` | How rendered deliverables look | Replace the bracketed prompts. |
| `voice.example/` | Your writing voice (a folder, not one file) | **Don't hand-fill the placeholders — run [`library/process/voice-setup.md`](../../process/voice-setup.md).** It builds your voice from your real writing (samples → mine corpus → taste interview → compile), because voice comes from examples, not self-description. |

Voice is the one that needs a process, not just form-filling — that's what `voice-setup.md` is for.
